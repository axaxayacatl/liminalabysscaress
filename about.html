01 / origin

SYNTHESIS COORDINATOR : Axayacatl

LAC exists as a liminal construct between fashion, system, and ritual object.

It is not a traditional brand but a controlled interface where intimacy is engineered and distributed through form.

The work is informed by the cybernetic thought of Donna Haraway, where bodies, machines, and identity converge into hybrid systems.

02 / structure

garments are treated as frameworks rather than clothing.

each piece operates as a lattice, a containment system for the body, balancing exposure and restriction within a digital logic.

designed for online game environments and emerging 3D printed fashion houses, the objects exist simultaneously as asset and artifact.

03 / philosophy

softness is not passive, it is engineered.
danger is aesthetic, but also structural.

LAC explores the tension between control and surrender through synthetic intimacy.

it draws from distributed cultures of open invention, decentralized studios, anonymous fabrication, and tool making as a shared ritual practice.
next →

04 / access

distribution is intentionally limited.

objects, files, and systems are released in controlled intervals.
access is not guaranteed, it is encountered.

positioned between digital economies and physical production, LAC interfaces with both virtual worlds and fabrication networks.
